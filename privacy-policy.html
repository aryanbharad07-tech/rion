Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

RION ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or interact with our services.

Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information: Name, phone number, email address, and any other details you provide via forms or quizzes.
  • Financial information: Income brackets, investment goals, risk preferences, and existing investments (only when voluntarily shared).
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited (via Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity).

How we use your information

  • To provide personalised advisory and recommendations.
  • To communicate with you (via email, phone, or WhatsApp) about your inquiries or plans.
  • To improve our website and services through analytics.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations (including AMFI/SEBI record‑keeping).

Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Creso (onboarding partner): To facilitate your investment onboarding, as per their privacy policy.
  • Service providers: CRM, analytics, and communication tools that help us operate (all contractually bound to keep data confidential).
  • Legal authorities: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data security

We implement industry‑standard security measures to protect your data. However, no online transmission is 100% secure; you share data at your own risk. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) and restrict access to personal information to authorised personnel only.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing us at rionmfd@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to withdraw consent at any time.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and for analytics. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings. Essential cookies are used for basic functionality; analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site.

Third‑party links

Our website may contain links to third‑party sites (including Creso). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Updates to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted on this page. Significant changes will be notified via email or a banner on our website.

Contact: For any privacy‑related questions, reach out to us at rionmfd@gmail.com
