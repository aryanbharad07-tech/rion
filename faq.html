Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money safe with RION?

We are a distributor, not a fund manager. Your investments are held directly with the AMC/Registrar. RION does not hold or manage your money.

What does RION charge?

Our advisory is commission‑based, paid by the AMC. We do not charge any direct client fees. (If you have a different model, update accordingly.)

What is Creso and why am I redirected there?

Creso is our technology partner that handles the secure onboarding, KYC, and transaction execution. Once you're redirected, you'll complete your investment setup with them under their terms.

Can I withdraw anytime?

Yes, mutual fund investments are liquid. You can redeem your units at any time subject to applicable exit load and tax implications.

Is this a SEBI Registered Investment Adviser service?

RION is an AMFI‑registered Mutual Fund Distributor (ARN-364240). We do not hold an Investment Adviser license. We guide and recommend, but all investment decisions are made by you.
