Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Is my money safe with RION?

We are a distributor, not a fund manager. Your investments are held directly with the AMC/Registrar. RION does not hold or manage your money. You have direct access to your investments at all times.

What does RION charge?

Our advisory is commission‑based, paid by the AMC. We do not charge any direct client fees. This means our advice is aligned with your long‑term success, not short‑term sales. You pay nothing out of pocket for our advisory services.

What is Creso and why am I redirected there?

Creso is our technology partner that handles the secure onboarding, KYC, and transaction execution. Once you're redirected, you'll complete your investment setup with them under their terms. They are a SEBI‑registered intermediary.

Can I withdraw anytime?

Yes, mutual fund investments are liquid. You can redeem your units at any time subject to applicable exit load and tax implications. There is no lock‑in period for most mutual funds (except ELSS).

Is this a SEBI Registered Investment Adviser service?

RION is an AMFI‑registered Mutual Fund Distributor (ARN-364240). We do not hold an Investment Adviser license. We guide and recommend, but all investment decisions are made by you. Our role is to educate, simplify, and help you make informed choices.

How do I get started?

Start by taking our 2‑minute KYIP quiz to understand your risk profile and investment goals. Then book a free consultation to discuss your personalised plan. You can also start investing directly via our partner Creso.

What if I have more questions?

Reach out to us anytime via email, phone, or WhatsApp. We're here to help.
