What it is

Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) are one of the most powerful tools for long‑term wealth creation. But the right SIP isn't just about picking a fund — it's about the right amount, frequency, and fund selection that aligns with your goal and risk profile.

Who it's for

Whether you're a salaried professional looking to start with ₹5,000 a month, or you have a larger surplus to deploy, our SIP planning helps you stay consistent and on track to achieve your financial milestones.

What's included

  • SIP amount calculation based on your goal
  • Fund selection for long‑term growth
  • Step‑up SIP strategy to increase contributions over time
  • Periodic performance reviews and rebalancing
  • Guidance on switching funds when needed
