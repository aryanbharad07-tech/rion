Building RION — from IIM Rohtak to impact

I founded RION with a clear mission: to build a transparent, investor-first platform that genuinely helps people grow their wealth. I wanted to move past traditional approaches and focus entirely on creating something that delivers true value.

India's underserved investing space is vast. Millions of hard‑working professionals in the 30‑45 age bracket are busy, often overwhelmed, and have little to no prior knowledge of mutual funds. They work hard for their money — and they deserve to see it grow, not just sit idle.

That's why I built RION — to bridge that gap. We provide simple, honest, and goal‑driven mutual fund advisory to help you generate real returns on your hard‑earned money.
